Siddagangaiah

GRASSROOTS ACTIVIST FOR THE COMMON MAN

From discontinuing his studies after 12th to managing a business of kitchen appliances at the age of 35 years, he has lived through many struggles. He aspires to change the system so that others have an easier time.

CITIZEN ACTIVISM

As an active volunteer with Dalit Sangarsh Samiti, he has taken up many causes of the community, to put up a united front in their fight for their constitutional rights.

POLITICIAN

In Aam Aadmi Party, he found an opportunity to learn and groom his leadership and organization skills, and applied them at the grassroots to take up a multitude of people’s issues in Malleshwaram. Rapidly developing over the years, he was appointed the Convenor of Malleshwaram assembly unit.

He has been at the forefront of many struggles taken up by AAP’s Bengaluru unit. He played major role in Bribe Free Karnataka helping large number of citizens get bribe-free services across government offices in Bangalore.

He was part of the Mission 40 campaign which focussed on reaching out to all residents of specific wards and educating them about the transformative power of honest politics and the benefits it can deliver to the masses.
