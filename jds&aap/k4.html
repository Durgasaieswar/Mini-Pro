Lingaraj Urs

FOUNDER OF WORLD RENOWNED IT COMPANY, DEVELOPED MODEL PANCHAYAT Grandson of Shri Devaraj Urs, Legendary Chief Minister of Karnataka

POLITICAL
Contested and won Panchayat Elections in Kannamangala in 2015.

Prime focus in the model village was on Education, Environment, Healthcare & Civic Infrastructure. His work positively impacted over 1200 students in 23 schools. He also led a waste segregation plan within his constituency as a model of waste management.Organised & successfully ran numerous health & environment related campaigns impacting 2 lakh people.Peformed and brought in parity in the property tax collection rates and ekhatha norms within the Panchayat.

PROFESSIONAL CAREER

Co-founded Genisys and expanded it to multiple countries. His professional journey saw him rise through the ranks of a management trainee, hardware salesman, operations manager, software project management, government contracting, all the way up to leading a multinational IT company for over 2 decades providing livelihood to 10,000 families.

COMMUNITY LEADER

President of the Federation of Resident Welfare Association, Communities and Establishment of Greater Whitefield ( Force GW). Led a unified voice of 30,000 residents of the area.
