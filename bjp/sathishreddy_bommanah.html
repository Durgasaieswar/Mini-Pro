Know your representative!!

SATHISH REDDY M has been the MLA of Bommanahalli for many years on a stretch.M. Satish Reddy is an Indian politician and member of the Bharatiya Janata Party. Reddy is a member of the Karnataka Legislative Assembly from the Bommanahalli constituency in Bangalore Urban district.
Since he took over as the MLA of Bommanahalli Constituency, he has strived to speed up the pace of the existing projects as well as implement new development works that are of a high priority. Some of them include: tarring of roads, construction of storm water drains, improving water and electricity supply. He also implementated the Bhagyalakshmi and the pension scheme.

Projects

Development of parks
Sathish ready also took up the task of developing the neighbourhood parks where people can enjoy morning and evening walks or even go for a job; children can play and so on. These include civil works, lighting, landscaping etc.
Development of lakes
The developmental and restoration work of the Puttenehalli Lake has been planned which was encroached upon by the BPL family. They will be relocated to Bettadasapura. Desiltation, strengthening of the earthen bund, construction of catch drains, strengthening of downstream drains, sedimentation filters, Bio-treatment plant, landscaping, toilets etc are some of the works for which planning is under progress.
Water supply and sewerage development
Around 430 electric borewells have been repaired and water supply to overhead tanks has been restored after cleaning. Under the Greater Bangalore Water supply project, a sum of Rs 74 crores has been sanctioned that includes providing clean drinking water of Cauvery to the houses, main and feeder supply network development etc. Extensive Work on the sewerage front has also been taken up. It includes development of house-to-house connections along with main and feeder sewerage line development the total cost of which is Rs 230 crores.
Construction of storm water drains
Bommanahalli Constituency has 4 major lakes – Sarakki and Puttenehalli Lake, Arekere Lake, Begur Lake and Madivala Lake. These lakes are interconnected by Raja Kaluve, which have been encroached upon thus reducing the area for water flow. As a result, there is always a massive flood in the surrounding areas during the rains. Extensive work on these storm water drains to the tune of Rs 5876 lakhs in 28 different locations have been taken up.
